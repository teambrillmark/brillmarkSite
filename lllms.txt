# BrillMark.com


> BrillMark is a leading Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) and A/B Test Development Agency. We offer high-velocity A/B testing, custom web development, analytics, and technical support to help businesses and agencies scale their digital performance.


## Core Services


- **A/B Test Development:** Custom split tests, multivariate tests, and personalization. We handle coding, platform setup (e.g., Convert, Optimizely), analysis, and QA.
- **Shopify & E-commerce Development:** Custom stores, mobile-first design, platform migration, and integrations for Shopify, BigCommerce, and others.
- **WordPress & Custom Web Development:** Themes, plugins, headless WordPress, and landing page creation.
- **Full-Stack Web Development:** End-to-end front and backend services for custom features and marketing campaigns.
- **UI/UX Design & Mockups:** User-centered, mobile-optimized designs with a focus on conversion optimization.
- **Analytics Setup & Support:** Implementation and ongoing support for platforms like GA4 and Mixpanel.
- **Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO):** Audits, hypothesis development, and strategic plans for measurable improvements.
- **Quality Assurance (QA):** Comprehensive testing across devices and browsers for functional and usability checks.
- **Dedicated Development Resources:** Flexible team integration via retainer, project, or block-of-hours models.


## Key Features


- **End-to-End Process:** From ideation and design to development, QA, deployment, and analysis.
