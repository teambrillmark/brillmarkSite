**BrillMark** is a specialized Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) and A/B Test Development Agency. The company focuses on providing high-velocity A/B testing, custom web development, CRO support, analytics, and technical services tailored for digital growth. ## Core Service Offerings

### 1. **A/B Test Development**
- **Custom setups:** Split tests, multivariate, personalization.
- **Full implementation:** Coding variations, setting up tests on platforms (e.g., Convert, Optimizely, VWO, Adobe Target, Kameleoon).
- **Analysis:** GA reporting, result interpretation, data-driven recommendations.
- **Quality Assurance:** Rigorous delivery across platforms & devices.

### 2. **Shopify & E-commerce Development**
- Custom online stores for Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Magento.
- Mobile-first, responsive design.
- Platform migration & third-party integrations.
- Advanced CRO for e-commerce: performance optimization, analytics integration.

### 3. **WordPress & Custom Web Development**
- Custom themes, plugins, headless WP, WooCommerce, Gutenberg.
- Landing page creation, site performance audits.
- CMS-Integation, scalable solutions, continuous support.

### 4. **Full-Stack & Custom Web Dev**
- End-to-end front and backend services.
- Landing pages, custom features, campaign-specific development.
- Marketing campaign support, unique feature creation, scalability.

### 5. **Design & Mockups**
- User-centered, brand-aligned, mobile-optimized UI/UX.
- A/B test mockups and iterative feedback process.
- Quick turnaround with conversion optimization focus.

### 6. **Analytics Setup & Support**
- GA4, Mixpanel, etc. implementation & goal/event tracking.
- Data analysis, actionable reporting, ongoing optimization.

### 7. **Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)**
- Audits, hypothesis development, implementation, and testing.
- Customized strategic plans for measurable improvement.

### 8. **Quality Assurance (QA)**
- Functional, usability, security, cross-device/browser testing.
- Detailed documentation, metric alignment, continuous improvement.

### 9. **Dedicated Development Resources**
- Flexible engagement: retainer, project, block-of-hours.
- Integration with existing client teams.
- Diverse technical skills, scalable support.
